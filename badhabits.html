BAD HABITS OF A PERSON

Bad habits are one of the biggest problems of modern society. Smoking and alcohol abuse are the most dangerous bad habits and bring enormous harm not only to people suffering from these habits-diseases, but also to the people around them and the whole society as a whole.

Bad habits: SMOKING

Smoking is one of the most common bad habits. Experts increasingly attribute this habit to drug addiction. Smokers inhale combustion products into the lungs and saturate the body with a whole set of toxic substances. Gorenje In addition, all these nasty things are inhaled by people around the smoker and very often they are also children. For them, this is also smoking, but passive.

Bad habits: ALCOHOLISM

Alcoholism has long been out of the concept of a bad habit, alcoholism is a disease. With alcoholism, the work of not only the human body is disrupted, but also psychological processes occur, leading to irreversible degradation of the personality. Treatment of alcoholism requires the participation of doctors of various specializations, including psychologists.

THE IMPACT OF BAD HABITS ON HUMAN HEALTH

Very often, people suffering from bad habits do not know about the impact of these habits on health or do not realize the danger that alcohol and tobacco pose. And it is not uncommon when a person, simply realizing what harm he does to himself and others, breaks up with bad habits.

The impact of smoking on human health:

The structure of nails and hair deteriorates, the skin color changes. Teeth turn yellow and weaken, bad breath appears. The vessels of the smoker become brittle and inelastic. Tobacco smoking contributes to the putrefaction of the gastrointestinal tract. The risk of stomach ulcers increases. Oxygen metabolism in the body is disrupted and, as a result, blood purification becomes difficult. Nicotine increases blood pressure. The likelihood of strokes, heart attacks, angina pectoris and other diseases of the cardiovascular system increases. The protection of the respiratory tract is violated, as a result of which smokers are more susceptible to diseases of the throat, bronchi and lungs, as well as more difficult to tolerate these diseases. Smoking contributes to the development of cancer. Smoking of pregnant women has a very negative effect on the health of the child. Very often such children lag behind in development and get sick more often.

PREVENTION OF BAD HABITS

It is not easy to fight with your bad habits, because fighting with yourself is a difficult matter. If you realize that tobacco and alcohol harm your life and the lives of people around you, then make every effort to cope with this harmful addiction. Read the literature, watch programs, contact specialists and you will definitely find a method and cope with this task.
